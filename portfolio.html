Tic-Tac-Toe Game

The Tic-Tac-Toe project is a classic game implemented using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It allows two players to take turns marking spaces on a 3x3 grid, aiming to get three of their symbols in a row, column, or diagonal. The game includes features such as highlighting the winning combination, resetting the board, and keeping track of the score.
